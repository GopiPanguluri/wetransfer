About this lesson

In week six we discuss how to structure the service delivery and operations of your business so that you can service your clients, deliver jaw dropping results while keeping your sanity and high profit margins.

Here you will learn how to set your client's expectations, work with contractors and clients as well as manage your own time for maximum efficiency.
