About this lesson

In week three we discuss the art and science of converting a total stranger to a paying customer with one phone call.

We teach you the mistakes most people make, how to sell like a master and we introduce you to our revolutionary sales script. From structure to questions to objection handling responses, this week covers sales from A to Z.
