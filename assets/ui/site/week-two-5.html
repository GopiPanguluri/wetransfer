About this lesson

In this video we show you why it's impossible to achieve success as the person you are today. We show you why there's a gap between who you are right now and the person you need to become to achieve your dreams. We then show you how to design that new character and grow into it so that achieving your dreams is effortless. 

Here's what we cover: 

  • What is Alchemy of self and why is it important?
  • The gap between who you are right now and who you need to become to achieve your dreams
  • Transcending yourself and making your character dance
  • Defining who you are at this present moment as well as the stories and beliefs you carry about the past
  • Defining your dream life, what you really want to achieve and the character you must play to achieve it
  • Rewriting your history, mythologizing yourself and creating your own self fulfilling prophecy of the future
